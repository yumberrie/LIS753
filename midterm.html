Sale On Used Books & More!

All proceeds go to the Palo Alto Library.

We have more than 50,000 used books, records, video cassettes, magazines, and more at our month sale. These items come from individual, estate, and company donations, as well as retired library materials.

If you wish to donate, please check out our donations page.

Where to find...

Most material will be in the Main room.

Youth and Teacher material: new room

Bargain books: rooms K6 and K7

Pricings

Hardbound books: $1
Paperbacks: $.50
Children’s books: $.25
Music, videos, toys: As marked

Sunday all bargain room books half-off.

Sunday bargain bags: $5 for grocery bag and everything you can fit inside!

Purchase gift certificates in $5 denominations.

Schedule

Weekends once a month.

Tickets are handed out between 8 and 11 AM.

Saturday 11 AM to 3 PM
Sunday Noon to 3 PM for Main room
11 AM to 3PM for Children’s and Bargain rooms

COVID Instructions

Due to limited space and health concerns, we are only allowing 160 people in the Main room at a time.

The number on the ticket reserves your spot in line, so you can leave and come back. If you are not there when your number is called, you forfeit your place in line.

If you miss the time to get a ticket, you can join the end of the line.

You may select 12 items per visit. After purchasing, please exit through the east door. (This item limit will drop if the line is gone after noon.)
